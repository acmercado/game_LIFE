ABOUT THE GAME

through i game, i wanted to simulate the concept of a person jumping over "unidentified objects" to replicate the idea of one overcoming "life-hurdles" to get further in his/her life.

on the other hand, the reddish-black background is to signify a person trying to get out of a "dystopic" environment to get their long-awaited "utopia".
